How easy is it to walk to school?

  • An equity report for Metro Vancouver prioritized schools that would benefit most from making it easier and safer for students to walk and bike to school.Scroll for more...

  • Surrey has a large number of of school-aged children, which, combined with low density and limited cyclist and pedestrian infrastructure, makes schools less walkable.

  • High-density housing and more developed walking and biking infrastructure in areas like downtown Vancouver or New Westminster make it easier to walk or bike to school.

  • Several affluent, low-density areas like southwest Vancouver, West Vancouver and South Surrey emerge as priorities, possibly because of high numbers of children combined with limited walking and biking infrastructure.
